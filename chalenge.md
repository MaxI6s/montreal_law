# Challenge Track #2 – Spellbook

## Close the Deal! – AI-Assisted Contract Negotiation

### The Challenge

Contract negotiation is one of the slowest and most frustrating parts of many business transactions.

Legal teams and business stakeholders often struggle with:
- endless redline exchanges
- difficulty understanding contract changes
- unclear negotiation priorities
- lack of visibility into remaining blockers
- inefficient workflows

Business leaders frequently rely on lawyers to translate legal markup into practical business implications. Meanwhile, teams spend enormous amounts of time negotiating clauses that often resolve in predictable ways.

The result:
- slower deals
- higher legal costs
- operational friction
- difficult collaboration between legal and business teams

### Your Objective

Build a tool that improves some aspect of the contract negotiation process. This challenge is intentionally broad and flexible. Teams may:
- focus deeply on one narrow problem
- or build a broader end-to-end negotiation workflow prototype

Potential directions include:
- Redline explainers that translate edits into plain language
- Contract review assistants
- Negotiation gap trackers
- Deal desk dashboards
- Clause risk analyzers
- “What-if” concession simulators
- AI negotiation co-pilots
- or entirely new approaches

The most important thing is identifying:
- a real user
- a real pain point
- and a meaningful solution

### Resources Provided

Spellbook will provide:
- sample fictional contracts (NDA & SaaS MSA)
- redlined versions of agreements
- scenario briefs explaining negotiation context and tensions
- and trial access to the Spellbook platform

Teams are welcome to use these materials directly or build entirely different solutions inspired by the broader problem space.
